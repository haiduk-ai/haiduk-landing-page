AI Agent

AI Agents are autonomous systems that can handle complex tasks, make decisions, and interact with your tools and data. They automate routine processes, saving time and boosting efficiency across departments.

Chatbot

Custom-trained chatbots that understand your business and data. They answer customer or internal queries instantly, reduce support load, and integrate smoothly into your existing communication systems.

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)

RAG systems pull real-time data from your internal documents and combine it with language models to deliver fast, accurate, and contextual answers to any query—ideal for knowledge-heavy workflows.

Computer Vision & OCR

These tools extract and interpret data from images, documents, or video. From reading invoices to detecting products in photos, they automate tasks that once required manual visual processing.
